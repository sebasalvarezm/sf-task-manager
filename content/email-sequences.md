# Valstone Outreach Email Sequences

This file contains the email skeletons for every portfolio subgroup. The Sourcing tool uses **Email 1** of the matched subgroup as the base draft, then swaps in three pieces:

1. **Hook sentence** — the sentence `I have studied {{account.name}} going back to the release of (INSERT PRODUCT).` is replaced by the tool's generated email hook.
2. **Core paragraph** — the middle paragraph (starts with "We are building a dedicated…") is replaced by the tool's personalized Outreach Paragraph.
3. **`[INSERT TOWN AND WEEK]`** — replaced with the company's town plus the week three weeks from today, e.g. "Johns Creek on the first week of August".

Merge fields like `{{first_name}}`, `{{account.name}}`, `{{sender.first_name}}`, and `{{sender.name}}` are filled by Salesforce and must be left untouched. The greeting line "Hi {{first_name}}," is intentionally omitted here (Salesforce/sequence tooling adds it).

Subgroup names below match the folder/file names in `content/groups/`. Note: **Equipment Dealers Rental has no email sequence yet.**
