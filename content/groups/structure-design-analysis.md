# Structure Design & Analysis

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Architectural and engineering firms and consultants (civil, structural, etc.), structural steel manufacturers.

**Workflows:** Building and infrastructure designing, structural analysis and load calculations, code checks, documentation.

**Types of Solutions:** CAD/BIM software, structural analysis tools, design collaboration platforms, reporting tools.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Structural Design and Analysis group. The vision is to become a long-term home for software that structural engineers rely on to model and design buildings and infrastructure, across analysis, code checks, concrete and foundation design, and documentation. In simple terms, we want to help engineers design safer structures faster, with fewer manual steps.
