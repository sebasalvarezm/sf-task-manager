# Manufacturing Aftermarket & Service

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Equipment and machinery OEMs (original equipment manufacturers) supporting their products after the sale, and their dealer and distributor networks served through the OEM. Note: software sold directly to dealers, rental companies, and fleet operators belongs to the Equipment Dealers, Rental & Maintenance group under Construction and Diversified Materials.

**Workflows:** Interactive parts catalogues, dealer and distributor enablement, service and maintenance documentation, warranty, spare parts ordering.

**Types of Solutions:** Interactive parts catalogue platforms, dealer and distributor portals, service documentation software, warranty management systems, spare parts e-commerce.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Manufacturing Aftermarket and Service group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs everything after the sale, across interactive parts catalogues, dealer and distributor enablement, service and maintenance documentation, warranty, and spare parts ordering. In simple terms, we help OEMs and their dealer networks find the right part, place the order, and keep equipment running, with fewer manual steps. A key step in this effort was our acquisition of Documoto, an interactive parts catalogue and aftermarket platform used by OEMs and their dealer networks, and we are actively adding more software across the service and aftermarket layer.
