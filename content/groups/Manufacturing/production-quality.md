# Manufacturing Production & Quality

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Tier 1 and Tier 2 manufacturers, aerospace and automotive suppliers, continuous manufacturers, testing labs.

**Workflows:** ERP and MRP, production scheduling and execution, MES, quality and first article inspection, statistical process control (SPC), lab and materials testing, traceability, compliance.

**Types of Solutions:** Manufacturing ERP and MRP, MES (manufacturing execution systems), first article inspection software, SPC and quality management systems, lab and materials testing software.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Manufacturing Production and Quality group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs the shop floor, across ERP and MRP, production scheduling and execution, MES, quality and first article inspection, statistical process control, lab and materials testing, traceability, and compliance. In simple terms, we help manufacturers run production reliably and prove quality, with fewer manual steps and less rework. Key steps in this effort were our acquisitions of Henning Software, an ERP for Tier 2 continuous manufacturers, and DISCUS Software, a first article inspection platform used by Tier 1 suppliers and OEMs in aerospace and automotive.
