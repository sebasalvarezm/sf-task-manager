# Manufacturing Engineering & Estimating

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Steel fabricators, HVAC manufacturers, job shops and custom manufacturers.

**Workflows:** Design and CAD/CAM programming, nesting and material optimization, estimating and quoting, simulation, program generation for the shop floor.

**Types of Solutions:** CAD/CAM software, nesting and yield optimization tools, estimating and quoting systems, simulation software.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Manufacturing Engineering and Estimating group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs the front end of the shop, across design and CAD/CAM programming, nesting and material optimization, estimating and quoting, simulation, and program generation for the floor. In simple terms, we help fabricators and manufacturers turn a drawing into an accurate quote and a ready-to-run program, with less waste and fewer manual steps. A key step in this effort was our acquisition of Shop Data, which provides CAD/CAM nesting software for steel fabricators and HVAC manufacturers, and we are actively adding more software across the engineering and estimating layer.
