# Industrial Terminals, Gate & Yard

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Industrial terminal operators, distribution and manufacturing sites, intermodal facilities, ports and rail yards.

**Workflows:** Gate automation and access control, yard management, appointment scheduling, terminal operations, dwell and throughput visibility.

**Types of Solutions:** Gate automation platforms, yard management systems (YMS), appointment scheduling tools, terminal operating systems, TMS (transportation management systems).

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Industrial Terminals, Gate and Yard group, part of a broader vision to become a long-term home for the software that moves industrial freight. For terminals, that spans gate automation and access control, yard management, appointment scheduling, terminal operations, and dwell and throughput visibility, with horizontal capabilities, TMS, workforce scheduling, and supplier sourcing, applying across modes. In simple terms, we help industrial sites move trucks and freight through the gate and across the yard, with fewer manual steps and less congestion. A key step in this effort was our acquisition of Nascent, a gate automation platform for industrial clients, and we are actively adding more software across terminal, gate, and yard operations.
