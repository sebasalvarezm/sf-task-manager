# Rail Logistics

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Class 1 and short line railroads, railcar owners and lessors, rail shippers, rail terminal operators.

**Workflows:** Transportation management and track-and-trace, railcar fleet management, crew and workforce scheduling, gate and terminal operations, asset visibility.

**Types of Solutions:** Rail TMS and track-and-trace software, railcar fleet management systems, crew and workforce scheduling platforms.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Rail Logistics group, part of a broader vision to become a long-term home for the software that moves industrial freight. For rail, that spans transportation management and track-and-trace, railcar fleet management, crew and workforce scheduling, gate and terminal operations, and asset visibility, with the same horizontal capabilities, TMS, yard and gate automation, workforce scheduling, and supplier sourcing, applying across modes. In simple terms, we help railroads and shippers keep equipment, crews, and freight moving, with fewer manual steps. Key steps in this effort were our acquisitions of GeoMetrix Logistics, a track-and-trace TMS in the railcar space, and PST, a crew scheduling platform for Class 1 railroads.
