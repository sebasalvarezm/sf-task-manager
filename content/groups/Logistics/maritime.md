# Maritime & Marine Logistics

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Vessel owners and operators, shipping lines moving industrial cargo, port and terminal operators, marine logistics providers.

**Workflows:** Ship-to-shore connectivity and communications, fleet and vessel operations, cargo documentation, port and terminal coordination, compliance.

**Types of Solutions:** Maritime communications platforms, vessel and fleet management software, cargo documentation systems, port and terminal coordination tools.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Maritime and Marine Logistics group, part of a broader vision to become a long-term home for the software that moves industrial freight. For maritime, that spans ship-to-shore connectivity and communications, fleet and vessel operations, cargo documentation, port and terminal coordination, and compliance, with horizontal capabilities, TMS, yard and gate automation, workforce scheduling, and supplier sourcing, applying across modes. In simple terms, we help operators moving industrial cargo keep vessels, crews, and freight connected and on schedule, with fewer manual steps. A key step in this effort was our acquisition of GTMaritime, a ship-to-shore communications platform for companies moving industrial cargo, and we are actively adding more software across maritime and marine logistics.
