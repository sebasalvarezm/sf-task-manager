# Protein & Livestock

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Livestock producers (swine, cattle, poultry), meat and dairy processors, animal health and feed providers.

**Workflows:** Herd and livestock management, animal health and feed, meat and dairy processing, traceability, cold chain.

**Types of Solutions:** Herd and livestock management software, MES (manufacturing execution systems) for meat and dairy processing, traceability systems, animal health and feed platforms.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Protein and Livestock group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs animal production and protein processing, across herd and livestock management, animal health and feed, meat and dairy processing, traceability, and cold chain. In simple terms, we help producers and processors raise animals and turn them into safe, traceable product, with fewer manual steps. Key steps in this effort were our acquisitions of PigKnows for swine management and Matrix Industrial Control Systems, an MES for meat and dairy processors, and we are actively adding more software across livestock and protein.
