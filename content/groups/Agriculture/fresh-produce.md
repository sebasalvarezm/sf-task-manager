# Fresh Produce

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Growers, packers and shippers, fresh produce distributors, cold storage operators, retail fresh supply chains.

**Workflows:** Grower and field operations, packing and grading, cold storage, distribution and logistics, food safety and traceability, customer experience.

**Types of Solutions:** Fresh produce ERP, packing and grading software, cold storage and inventory management systems, food safety and traceability platforms.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Fresh Produce group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs fresh produce operations, across grower and field operations, packing and grading, cold storage, distribution and logistics, food safety and traceability, and customer experience. In simple terms, we help growers, packers, and shippers move fresh product from field to retailer reliably, with less spoilage and fewer manual steps. A key step in this effort was our acquisition of Prophet, an ERP for the fresh produce industry, and we are actively expanding our footprint across fresh produce and the wider food and beverage supply chain.
