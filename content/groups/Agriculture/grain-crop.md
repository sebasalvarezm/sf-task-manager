# Grain & Crop

## Group Overview

**End Markets:** Crop farms, grain elevators and merchandisers, feed mills, grain processors, agricultural retailers and co-ops.

**Workflows:** Farm accounting and management, grain elevator and merchandising operations, storage and handling, feed milling, process control.

**Types of Solutions:** Farm accounting software, grain elevator ERP, commodity merchandising systems, feed mill process control and automation.

---

## Core Outreach Paragraph

We are building a dedicated Grain and Crop group. The vision is to become a long-term home for the software that runs crop production and grain handling, across farm accounting and management, grain elevator and merchandising operations, storage and handling, feed milling, and process control. In simple terms, we help growers, elevators, and mills move grain from field to processor reliably, with fewer manual steps. Key steps in this effort were our acquisitions of Red Wing Software for crop farm accounting, Vertical Software for grain elevator ERP, and WEM Automation for feed mill process control.
